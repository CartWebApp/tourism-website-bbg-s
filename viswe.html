Trains/metro

Trains and metros are a key way to get from major city to major city. Some will be longer than others. But, it is mostly used by the average working class.

Vehicles

Cars are plentiful in Sweden. But there is also a large cycling group in Sweden.

 
 
Cruises and Boat Trips

Boat trips happen all around the coast of Sweden. From the most Southern tip, to the most Northern part. Cruises in Sweden are also beautiful because of the scenery that no one can ever picture.

Lodging

Lodging options in Sweden can be exciting. There is even a floating hotel! There are also historic places to stay. If you even feel a little bit brave, there is even an ice hotel!
